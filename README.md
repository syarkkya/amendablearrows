### 🏹 Amendable Arrows

Tired of manually crafting tipped arrows or wasting expensive lingering potions? **Amendable Arrows** introduces a seamless way to infuse your arrows with potion effects using the *Amendments* mod's liquid cauldrons. It's a fast, intuitive, and immersive way to prepare your ammunition for any battle.

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/syarkkya/amendablearrows/refs/heads/main/assets/amendablearrows/separator-light.png" alt="Separator Light">
</p>

### ✨ Features

- **Liquid Infusion** 🧪 — Turn regular arrows into powerful tipped arrows using any potion-filled cauldron from *Amendments*.
- **Complex Alchemy** 🔮 — Create unique arrows with mixed effects that are impossible to obtain in vanilla Minecraft.
- **Smart Scaling** 🧠 — The infusion process intelligently calculates effect durations based on the mixture ratio in the cauldron, ensuring perfectly balanced results.
- **Balanced Efficiency** ⚖️ — Infuse up to 16 arrows at once with a single click, consuming exactly 1 level of liquid from the cauldron per batch to keep gameplay fair.

### ⚙️ How it works

Using **Amendable Arrows** is as simple as it gets:

1. **Prepare your Cauldron** — Fill an *Amendments* liquid cauldron with your desired potion or mixture.
2. **Hold your Arrows** — Keep a stack of regular arrows in your main or off-hand.
3. **Right-Click** — Simply right-click the cauldron.
4. **Infuse!** — Your regular arrows will be instantly transformed into tipped arrows, and the cauldron's liquid level will decrease by one.
